The foundation of the brand's identity, using a clean and minimal palette.

#### Summary:
> - **Base Colors (Black, White, Blue)** are the universal core for all applications.
> - **Extended Palettes** are tailored for specific product lines (Urban for Road Bikes, Sports for Sport Bikes) to communicate their unique characteristics.
> - Colors can be used alone or in combination with their designated partner palette.
> - Digital Blue (`#2b7dde`) serves as the key functional color for web elements, bridging the base and extended palettes

## 3.Typography System Summary

### Primary Font: Helvetica Neue
**Usage:** Headlines, dominant statements, and word mark of the logo.

### Secondary Font: DM Mono
**Usage:** Sublines, continuous text, call-to-actions, technical product information, and all other text applications.
